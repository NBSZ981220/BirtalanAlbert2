REVIEWS ABOUT US

Christian Bale

actor

I drank the best coffee of my life here. The service is first class. I recommend everyone to try all the coffee in a row. My partner and I came here. He liked it too. If we lived in this city, we would probably have coffee here every day. That would be our headquarters.

Amanda Grey

actress

As an actress, I have traveled around the world. I have already tried Egyptian cat coffee. It was very special. That, in turn, is delicious. I don't think I need to say more than that. The prices are absolutely fine. The service is perfect. What else do you need? Be sure to give it a try if you go for it. Worth it.

Bob Medley

singer

As a pretty famous singer, I slowly got used to being treated differently. The waiters start taking photos with me. That's not what happened here. The waiters are very conscious. They know the guest is the first, and they treated me like that. They brought the requested coffee, which was very delicious. They were kind all along and also made sure I got a place where photographers couldn’t disturb me.

Josh Burrow

Dragons Imagination, guitarist

As a guitarist, concentration is very important to me. The best basis for this is good coffee. I was here when we were performing in town with the band. I really liked the place. Waiters pay special attention to everyone. And the coffee is the most delicious, even though we have already performed in many places.
